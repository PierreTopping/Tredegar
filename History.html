Town & Archive History

Tracing the industrial legacy of Tredegar and our digital footprint.

1890

Medical Aid Society

The foundation of the Tredegar Medical Aid Society, providing a localized healthcare system that would inspire Aneurin Bevan's UK-wide NHS.

1991

JANET Network Launch

Tredegar makes its first digital appearance on the academic JANET system, archiving local town records.

1994

Community BBS

The system transitions to a dial-up Bulletin Board System, hosting local chat, digital history, and early text forums.

1998

The World Wide Web

www.tredegar.co.uk launches globally, expanding into the largest web repository for the town with thousands of media files.
