About Us

The story, mission, and dedication behind the digital preservation of Tredegar's heritage.

Mission Statement

  • To supply news and information on the town of Tredegar.
  • To supply historically correct information on the town of Tredegar.
  • To help in any way it can to aid the rejuvenation of this great town.
  • To support local businesses, clubs, and individuals.
  • To be free from any external influences via individuals, businesses, council, or whatever.

Our History

We started to provide news and information on Tredegar via computer in early 1991 using the JANET system. This was the network environment used primarily by universities before the widespread commercial availability of the Internet. The very first correspondence was received in February 1991 from an individual in Texas, USA, seeking information about a relative they had lost contact with.

Later that year, we launched a version of the site accessible via a Bulletin Board System (BBS), allowing community members to connect directly using standard computer modems. The system ran reliably using this configuration for over three years.

With the onset of the modern Internet, we migrated the service in approximately 1995. Following the wider rollout of broadband networks to the UK in 2004, the website is now proudly hosted on dedicated infrastructure physically located right here within Tredegar.

The current archival platform includes over 10,000 historic photos of the town alongside more than 600 video clips highlighting Tredegar's evolution throughout the decades. We continuously expand this vast multimedia collection day by day.

In 2026, the website underwent a major update, introducing new team members to carry forward the foundational work that was started all the way back in 1991. For the time being, the archive remains closed to the general public with access provided by invitation only, though plans are actively underway to open up public areas of the website in the near future.

Authorship & Support

Across its many digital generations and formats, this site has been primarily financed, edited, and maintained by a single dedicated individual. However, in recent years, several individuals have generously contributed their personal time to provide editorial content and technical assistance in keeping the system running smoothly.
