Chronicles of Tredegar

Exploring the rich industrial, social, and structural evolution of our community through deep-dive record preservation.

Expanded Digital Research Archive

Our historical database houses thousands of scanned documents, regional mapping assets, transport ledger logs, and media files relating to the local community framework.

Core Historical Focus Areas

The collection focuses heavily on protecting vulnerable media formats from decaying over time. Our ongoing efforts include indexing:

  • Industrial Heritage: Ironworks ledgers, coal mining charts, and employment registers from the height of the valley's manufacturing era.
  • Infrastructure Layouts: Town blueprints, early railway development maps, and changing civil architecture footprints.
  • Community Forums & Media: Digitized photographic collections, local circular prints, and community group interactions from early local networks.
