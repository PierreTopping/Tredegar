Our Digital Journey

Established as a historical pillar of Welsh internet history, this platform is the largest source of media and documentation dedicated to Tredegar. From its inception as an academic node to its current evolution as a static archive repository, our mission remains to safeguard the incredible stories, industrial triumphs, and social movements of our valley town.

NHS Heritage Site

Aneurin Bevan & The Birth of the NHS

Tredegar stands globally recognized as the true crucible of modern social healthcare. It was right here that Aneurin "Nye" Bevan structuralized his vision for the British National Health Service (NHS). Drawing direct institutional blueprint designs from the community-funded Tredegar Medical Aid Society, Bevan ensured that medical support would be free at the point of delivery for an entire nation.

Community Forums & Archive

Our classic chat and community forums served for years as a vital digital meeting grounds for local residents and the global Tredegar diaspora. While the traditional live forum databases are undergoing migration to prevent structural spam, the core conversational memories, threads, and photo entries remain protected within our architectural storage banks.

Our Architecture Lineage

  • 1991 – 1994: Founded on the academic JANET System (Joint Academic Network) node architecture.
  • 1994 – 1998: Maintained and operated as a local community Dial-up BBS (Bulletin Board System) network.
  • 1998 – Present: Launched officially onto the modern World Wide Web (WWW) framework.
