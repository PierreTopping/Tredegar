Aneurin "Nye" Bevan

Father of the NHS

Born right here in Tredegar, Aneurin Bevan spearheaded the creation of the National Health Service in 1948. He famously based its revolutionary structure directly on the local Tredegar Medical Aid Society, ensuring that the care principles pioneered by our town's miners became the blueprint for healthcare across the entire nation.
